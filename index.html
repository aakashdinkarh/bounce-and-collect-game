Welcome to the Bounce and Collect Game!

Your objective is to collect as much as possible randomly generated points while mastering the effects of gravity and elasticity.

Click or tap anywhere within the field to launch the ball along a direct path. The ball will follow a straight path till that point and then projectile motion after that point and be influenced by elasticity and the force of gravity.

As the ball navigate the field, it will collect the score points. Challenge yourself by adjusting the gravity (g) and elasticity (e) to test different gameplay scenarios.

Note: Points will be collected only after the ball has started following a projectile motion.

In this mode, there are no winning or losing. Enjoy the freedom to practice and improve your skills.

Challenge your friend(s) in a thrilling head-to-head competition. Enter the names of the players. The player with the highest total score at the end will be declared as the winner.

Test your skills against the computer. Enter your name and select the number of rounds you wish to play. Can you outsmart the CPU and secure victory?

Get ready to put your skills to test, strategies your moves, and collect as many points as you can!

Have fun and enjoy the game!
